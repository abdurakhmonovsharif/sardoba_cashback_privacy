Account Deletion

This page explains how users can request account deletion in the Sardoba mobile application.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Sardoba mobile app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the action

What data is deleted

  • User profile information
  • Transaction history
  • Bonus and cashback data
  • Device and session data

Data retention

The user's phone number is retained for up to 90 days for legal and fraud-prevention purposes. After 90 days, the phone number is permanently deleted from our systems.
