Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Sardoba (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use the Sardoba mobile application (“the App”).

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use the App:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone number – used for account registration and login via SMS verification.
  • Name and surname (optional) – for displaying your profile.
  • Date of birth (optional) – used for profile personalization and bonus/marketing scenarios.
  • Referral code (optional) – used for referral and bonus programs if provided.

1.2 Technical Information

  • Device information (such as device model and operating system version).
  • Application diagnostics and crash logs used to improve stability and performance.

1.3 Push Notifications

  • Push notification token (FCM Device Token) – used to send notifications about bonuses, updates, and service messages.

1.4 Loyalty and Cashback Information

  • Loyalty card details (card number and/or tracking data).
  • Cashback balance and transaction history.

1.5 Location Data (Optional)

With your permission, we may collect approximate location data to show nearby branches and provide map functionality.

1.6 Camera and Gallery Access (Optional)

If you choose to update your profile picture:

  • Camera access – to take a photo.
  • Gallery access – to select an existing image.

We do not access your photos or videos without your action. Only the image you select is used for profile functionality.

2. How We Use Information

  • to create and authenticate user accounts;
  • to display and manage user profiles;
  • to provide loyalty card, cashback balance, and transaction history;
  • to send push notifications related to services, bonuses, and updates;
  • to improve app performance and fix technical issues;
  • to prevent fraud and misuse when necessary.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services to support certain features, including:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) – for push notifications;
  • iiko – for loyalty cards, cashback, and transaction management (if used by the App infrastructure);
  • Yandex MapKit – for map and branch location functionality.

These services may process limited technical identifiers in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only:

  • with service providers necessary to operate App features;
  • when required by law or lawful government requests.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active or as required for legal and technical purposes. Upon your request to delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your data in accordance with applicable laws and technical limitations.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • request account deletion;
  • update or correct your personal information;
  • disable push notifications via device settings;
  • contact us with questions regarding data processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@sardoba.uz

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will become effective when posted. The latest version will always be available at the provided URL.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@sardoba.uz
