Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Sardoba Restaurant ("we", "our", "us") operates the Sardoba mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from users of our App:

  • Phone number – for registration and login.
  • Name, birth date, gender (optional) – for user profile.
  • FCM Device Token – to send push notifications.
  • Loyalty card data (card number & track) for use with iiko loyalty system.
  • Cashback and transaction history.
  • Device information (model, OS version, analytics data if enabled).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used only for:

  • Account creation and authentication.
  • Sending SMS verification codes.
  • Managing loyalty points and balances.
  • Displaying cashback transactions in the app.
  • Sending push notifications (bonuses, order info, updates).
  • Improving our services.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party providers:

  • iiko Cloud – to manage loyalty cards and cashback.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) – to deliver push notifications.

These services may collect device identifiers and usage data according to their own Privacy Policies.

4. Data Security

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with unauthorized third parties. All sensitive information is transmitted securely using encrypted communication channels.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You may request the following at any time:

  • Deletion of your account.
  • Correction of your personal data.
  • Stopping push notifications.

To submit a request, contact us at:

Email: support@sardoba.uz

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy when needed. Updated versions will always be available at this URL.
