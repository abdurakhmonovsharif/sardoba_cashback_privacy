Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-03

This Privacy Policy explains how Sardoba ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Sardoba mobile application (the "App").

Important: The App is not a financial service. It does not provide payments, banking, money transfers, lending, investments, or order processing. The App only displays loyalty points and a catalog. Points are not money and cannot be withdrawn. Any real payments or accounting happen outside the App (for example, in a POS system).

1. App Features and What Data They Need

  • Account and login (OTP): phone number; optional name/surname and date of birth.
  • Loyalty points: loyalty card identifiers and points balance/history to show your rewards.
  • QR code display: the App can show a QR code linked to your account for loyalty use.
  • Catalog: shows items and offers; no orders or payments are created in the App.
  • Branches and map: optional location data to show nearby branches and navigation.
  • Notifications: push token to send service updates and points alerts.
  • Profile photo: camera or gallery access if you choose to upload a photo.
  • Referral: referral code to track invitations and award points.
  • Support: optional call or email to contact support.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone number (for registration and login).
  • Name/surname (optional, for profile display).
  • Date of birth (optional, for personalization).
  • Referral code (optional, for loyalty program).

2.2 Loyalty and Points Data

  • Loyalty card details (card number or internal identifiers).
  • Points balance and points history (transactions related to loyalty points).

2.3 Technical and Diagnostic Data

  • Device information (model, OS version).
  • App diagnostics, crash logs, and error reports.

2.4 Push Notifications

  • Push notification token (to deliver points updates and service messages).

2.5 Location Data (Optional)

If you grant permission, we use location data to show nearby branches and map/navigation features. You can deny permission and still use the App.

2.6 Camera and Gallery (Optional)

If you choose to update your profile photo, we request camera or gallery access. We do not access your photos or videos without your action.

3. How We Use Information

  • create and authenticate user accounts;
  • display and manage profiles;
  • show loyalty points balance and history;
  • display catalog and branch information;
  • send notifications about points and service updates;
  • improve App stability and fix issues;
  • prevent fraud or misuse when necessary.

4. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not process payments or orders in the App.
  • We do not collect bank card numbers or bank account details.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not use your data for cross-app tracking or third-party advertising profiling.

5. Sharing of Information

We share information only when necessary to operate the App:

  • with service providers that support App functionality;
  • when required by law or a valid legal request.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notifications.
  • iiko — loyalty program and points data management.
  • Yandex MapKit — maps, branch locations, and navigation.

These providers process limited technical identifiers according to their own privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

We keep data while your account is active, or as required for legal, security, or technical purposes. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

  • request deletion of your account and personal data;
  • update or correct your personal information;
  • disable notifications through device settings;
  • contact us with questions about data processing.

10. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes become effective when posted.
